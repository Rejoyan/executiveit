Why to choose

  • Jason London
  • Network Specialist
  • 14

In this career, you’ll interact with people at all levels of the organization and will need to simply explain complex topics. Good communication skills are a must! You should enjoy working with others and have great customer service skills. In addition, you’ll need to problem solve, think critically, and think quickly. When a network is down on a high-stakes day, it’s your time to shine, but you’ll need to withstand the pressure and act fast. Ready to earn a reliable salary, use your people skills, and get a skillset that many companies rely on? It only takes a few months to start your new IT career. Get the certification training and hands-on experience you need to launch your career in system administration

We may not need Santa Claus on Christmas but we certainly need System Administrators on each and every day of our lives to help us feel better.

As computers grow in importance in virtually every sector of the economy, system administrators play a vital role in making sure computer systems are running smoothly and securely. They are likely to be an important part of the IT sector for the foreseeable future.
